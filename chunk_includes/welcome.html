Welcome to the experiment!

In this experiment, you will evaluate conversations in English and assign a rating from a scale of 1-7. It will take approximately 20 minutes to complete.

Part One: Questionnaire and Consent to Participate (1 minute)

Give some information about yourself and your level of English.

Part Two: Practice Session (2 minutes)

Become familiar with the style of experiment with a few "practice" conversations.

Part Three: Experiment (approx. 15 minutes)

Evaluate conversations in English happening via Whatsapp.

Part Four: Feedback (1 minute)

Tell us about your experience with the experiment.


Note: If you have ever been diagnosed with an impairment that might affect your comprehension of written language, please DO NOT complete the experiment.

(Examples of impairments: developmental dyslexia, acquired brain damage, specific language impairment, learning disability/ developmental language disorder.)

This does NOT include physical abnormalities such as deafness or stuttering.
